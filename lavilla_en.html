The villa

Located just a few minutes from the centre of Carcès, Lei Cadé is a wonderful villa of 155m2 ideally located in a quiet area at a green zone edge where only cicadas could disturb your siesta.​

On a terraced land of 2500m2 the property has several terraces allowing you to enjoy your family meals or the chaises longues under the sun of the south. The villa also has a beautiful private infinity pool and a petanque strip.

The house can accommodate up to eight people and offers all modern comfort in a Provençal setting.​

On the ground floor the kitchen opens onto a large living room with access to the two upper terraces. You are free to choose either to enjoy your meal at the outdoor table or take the aperitif comfortably installed in the garden salon. On the same floor is the master suite is composed of a double bedroom, a separate dressing room and a bathroom. On the upper floor is the three double bedrooms and the bathroom.
