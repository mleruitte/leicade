Franco-Belgian couple, residents in the Netherlands for many years, we fell in love with Carcès and its region. This property particularly seduced us with its location. We have fitted it out little by little and it is always with great pleasure that we return there to spend our holidays there.

Located in the village of Carcès in the heart of Var Lei Cadé is the perfect villa for your family holiday in a quiet and relaxing environment.

Situated in the heart of Green Provence this is the ideal starting point to discover the beautiful landscapes of the area and the many cultural and touristic sites.

Whether you are an amateur or an experienced athlete, hiking or water sports lover, you will find what you are looking for in the surroundings of the house.
